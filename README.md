### Revenue & Marketing Intelligence Platform

RevenuePulse AI is an enterprise-grade Revenue & Marketing Intelligence Platform designed to help organizations analyze revenue performance, marketing effectiveness, customer behavior, and business growth trends through interactive dashboards, AI-driven insights, and predictive analytics.

Built with modern web technologies, the platform transforms raw business data into actionable intelligence, enabling data-driven decision-making for executives, analysts, marketing teams, and revenue leaders.
